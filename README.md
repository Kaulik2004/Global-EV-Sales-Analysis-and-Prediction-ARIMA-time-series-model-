# Global-EV-Sales-Analysis-and-Prediction-ARIMA_time_series

## Why EV Sales Prediction is Necessary Today

In the current global landscape, the transition from Internal Combustion Engine (ICE) vehicles to Electric Vehicles (EVs) is no longer just an environmental choice but a strategic necessity. There are several critical reasons why predicting EV sales is vital:

**Petroleum Scarcity and Geopolitical Stability**: As global petroleum reserves face long-term depletion and supply chains become increasingly vulnerable to geopolitical tensions, reliance on fossil fuels poses a significant economic risk. Predicting EV adoption helps nations plan for energy security by shifting demand toward diverse, renewable electricity sources.
* **Infrastructure Planning**: Governments and private entities need accurate sales forecasts to strategically deploy charging stations. Knowing where and how fast the shift is happening ensures that the "range anxiety" of new users is addressed before it becomes a bottleneck.
